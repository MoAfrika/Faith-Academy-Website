Our Mission

We Turn Potential Into Professionals.

Youth unemployment is our country's greatest challenge. But with the right skills, it becomes our greatest opportunity. Faith Academy equips young South Africans with practical, job-ready certifications in the fastest-growing industries.

"A thriving South Africa where every young person has the skills, confidence, and opportunity to build a sustainable livelihood and uplift their community."
